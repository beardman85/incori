Our Residential Habilitation services offer different living arrangements to support independent living. **Continuous Home:** shift staff provide around-the-clock care and teaching in a provider-controlled home. **Host Home:** individuals live with an agency provider employee, sharing daily life and community activities. **Shared Living:** participants live with a contractor, learning independent living skills in a family-like environment.
